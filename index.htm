Welcome!

Quick introduction before we get into our game. Together, Jeff Huang and Isaac Lee, we are Amadaeus Stradivari. Inspired by various different developers and strategies when it comes to the development of web games. With the help of several online tutorials.

Instructions

The premise of our game is fairly simple. There are goblins which are going to try and hit you with their laser blasters. It is your job to destroy the goblins.

Controls

W - Move forward
A - Move to the left
S - Move backwards
D - Move to the right
I - Dash forward
J - Rotate counter-clockwise
K - Dash backward
Spacebar - Fire
